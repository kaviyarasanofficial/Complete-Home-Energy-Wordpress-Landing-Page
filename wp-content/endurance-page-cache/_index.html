How is the Boiler Grant Free?

The provision of free boiler grants might raise questions about its affordability. However, the funding for these grants comes through the Government’s ECO3 scheme, which mandates the major energy companies, including British Gas, E.ON, SSE, npower, EDF Energy, and ScottishPower, to allocate funds. The primary goal is to alleviate fuel poverty and assist households in reducing their carbon footprint.

This allocated funding is utilized for the installation of new, energy-efficient heating systems in households that stand to benefit the most. If you meet the specified criteria, you could qualify to receive a new boiler and central heating system without incurring any costs.

How long does the installation process take?

It’s natural to be eager to know when your new boiler will be installed and your home will be warm, especially if your current boiler is causing issues.

Typically, we aim to have your new boiler installed and operational within 2-3 weeks from the time you submit your application. In some cases, this timeframe may be even shorter. However, if there are additional requirements from third-party services, such as the need for a new gas supply to your property, the process may take a bit longer. It’s important to note that the expenses related to a new gas supply won’t be covered by the scheme, although alternative funding options may be available to address these costs.

Who is eligible to apply for a Boiler Grant?

Homeowners residing in a property with a boiler that is over 6 years old can qualify if they receive one or more of the specified benefits. Eligible benefits include Universal Credit, Pension Credit, ESA, JSA, Income Support, Working Tax Credits, Child Tax Credits, AFIP, Attendance Allowance, Carers Allowance, DLA, PIP, War Pensions Mobility Supplement, Severe Disablement Allowance, Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit, Constant Attendance Allowance, or Child Benefits.
